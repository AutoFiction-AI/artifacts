Nursing homes in 2026 appear to be very drab and lackluster, with beige walls, beige furniture, apathetic inhabitants and workers. However underneath this lies a masterful scheme that no one has suspected…until now! What may appear as a group of cognitively and functionally impaired octogenarians are actually secret late night underground drug dealers, peddling their own prescriptions in secret to the outside world and making bank. They hide their skill and function to all except their drug ring members. Their operations have gone unnoticed, until one kind and hardworking nurse notices something odd and stumbles accidentally upon this operation. Will she out the group, ruining their only connection to the outside world and possibly landing them in worse straits than a nursing home, or will she keep lent knowing that all the medications are dispensed under her name.
