Years after Operation Strix helped prevent war between Westalis and Ostania, tensions between the two nations are rising once again. Now, Anya has grown into a capable intelligence operative under Loid's guidance. She is assigned to investigate a suspicious figure within Ostania's leadership, and is shocked to discover it is Damian himself working in secret to stop extremists from stirring up conflicts. The two former classmates are forced into a cautious alliance where neither fully trusts the other, and they need to uncover a hidden conspiracy, then decide whether the bond that first connected them as children might be the key to keeping the peace once more.

Canon background (for readers unfamiliar with the original story): During a period of tense relations between the rival nations of Westalis and Ostania, master spy Loid Forger created a fake family as part of Operation Strix, a mission intended to prevent war by getting close to powerful political leader Donovan Desmond. Unbeknownst to him, his new wife Yor was secretly an assassin and his adopted daughter Anya was a telepath. Despite their hidden identities, the three gradually formed a genuine bond while Anya's friendship with Donovan's son Damian helped maintain a fragile connection between the two countries.

Tone: action comedy-drama in the spirit of the original — espionage tension, action set-pieces, and genuine emotional stakes coexist with humor, warmth, and absurdity.
