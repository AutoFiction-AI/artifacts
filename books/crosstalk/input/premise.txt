When the Bebop crew returns to their usual docking spot behind a Mars noodle shop, they find a sleek, unfamiliar ship already parked there. Annoyed and suspicious, they try to track down the owner, only for strange clues to start appearing: a bounty for someone who looks like a younger Jet under a different name, an old logistics alias linked to Spike quietly triggered by the ship's ID, and a waitress insisting she remembers Faye from a gambling table years before Faye supposedly woke from cryosleep. Determined to reclaim their spot, the crew follows the mysterious pilot across Mars in a chaotic chase filled with ridiculous cons, near-misses, and escalating trouble. But the deeper they dig, the more it reveals small secrets none of them ever mentioned: side jobs, forgotten encounters, and strange moments where their lives may have crossed long before the Bebop. By the time they finally catch the ship's owner, they realize the parking incident was never random. Someone deliberately brought them together to prove their paths had already been intertwined long before fate put them on the same ship.
