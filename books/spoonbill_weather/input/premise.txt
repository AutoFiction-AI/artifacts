At a mangrove bird camp built on stilts above blackwater, a disgraced field guide becomes obsessed with a rumor that a luminous eel surfaces only during the spoonbills' mating frenzy. To stay in the creeks at night she begins sleeping with the married mosquito pilot who can steal fuel for her and with his wife, the camp's bat surveyor, while hiding that the first eel she ever saw may have been one she helped smuggle out of a biotech tank.
