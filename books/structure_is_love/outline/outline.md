# Novel Outline

## Primary Narrative Engine
**Primary engine:** literary fiction + character study + coming-of-age.

**Secondary engine:** sports-pressure drama (non-thriller, non-mystery).

**Consequence model:** chapters are driven mainly by psychological and relational consequences (grief displacement, bodily cost, trust erosion, class shame, changing authority), with tournament results acting as pressure points rather than the core meaning system.

## Chapter Plan (18 Chapters)

### Chapter 1 - Ghost Routine
- **Texture/mechanism:** high-intensity training day; present-tense bodily immediacy.
- **Objective:** Anika survives her first full training morning since her mother's death.
- **Conflict:** Ravi imposes a punishing regimen while Anika is still numb and dissociative.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika discovers that physical pain can temporarily mute grief and agrees to dawn sessions.

### Chapter 2 - School Face
- **Texture/mechanism:** low-intensity social pressure; dialogue-heavy school chapter.
- **Objective:** Anika tries to pass as normal at school.
- **Conflict:** Exhaustion, teacher scrutiny, and Zoe's invitation collide with Ravi's strict training schedule.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika chooses training over friendship; Zoe feels rejected and Anika's social world narrows.

### Chapter 3 - Inventory of a Kitchen
- **Texture/mechanism:** aftermath and inventory; reflective domestic chapter.
- **Objective:** Help Ravi clear Maya's belongings for donation.
- **Conflict:** Ravi wants speed and silence; Anika cannot let go of specific objects and memories.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika secretly keeps Maya's watch and notebook, beginning a private ledger of time, hunger, and pain.

### Chapter 4 - Ladder Match
- **Texture/mechanism:** compressed competition chapter.
- **Objective:** Win her first borough ladder match.
- **Conflict:** An older opponent targets her inexperience; grief flashes destabilize focus.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika wins but physically crashes; Ravi praises toughness, teaching her that distress only counts if converted into performance.

### Chapter 5 - Hairline
- **Texture/mechanism:** preparation/dread without payoff.
- **Objective:** Prepare for city qualifiers.
- **Conflict:** Shin pain worsens; nurse and coach warn rest, Ravi dismisses it as weakness.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika lies about pain and keeps training, setting up a preventable injury.

### Chapter 6 - Paperwork
- **Texture/mechanism:** information-turn chapter.
- **Objective:** Complete hardship forms for tournament fee relief.
- **Conflict:** Bureaucratic paperwork reveals unresolved hospital debt from Maya's treatment.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika reframes squash as economic rescue and doubles her training burden.

### Chapter 7 - Birthday Pizza
- **Texture/mechanism:** quiet social chapter with humor and release.
- **Objective:** Attend Zoe's birthday without discussing squash.
- **Conflict:** Anika cannot stop tracking calories and practice time; she feels disloyal while laughing.
- **Consequence/state change:** She admits aloud that she misses her mother every hour; Zoe re-enters as emotional ally.

### Chapter 8 - Qualifier Day
- **Texture/mechanism:** high-intensity tournament chapter.
- **Objective:** Qualify for the regional training pool.
- **Conflict:** Acute shin pain and explicit pressure from Ravi to finish no matter what.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika qualifies, then collapses; diagnosis confirms a tibial stress fracture and mandatory rest.

### Chapter 9 - Rest Is Not Rest
- **Texture/mechanism:** aftermath and psychic recoil.
- **Objective:** Endure six weeks off-court.
- **Conflict:** Without drills, grief and anger flood back; Ravi withdraws into silence and overtime shifts.
- **Consequence/state change:** Nightly voicemails from Maya become Anika's substitute ritual, deepening dependence on the past.

### Chapter 10 - Compliance
- **Texture/mechanism:** protagonist acted upon by institutions.
- **Objective:** Begin physical therapy and retain tournament eligibility.
- **Conflict:** Therapist Tess requires reduced load and mental-health check-ins; Ravi tries to override protocol.
- **Consequence/state change:** The program threatens suspension if rules are broken, limiting Ravi's unilateral control.

### Chapter 11 - Locker 214
- **Texture/mechanism:** slow-burn discovery.
- **Objective:** Empty Maya's hospital storage locker to avoid fees.
- **Conflict:** Hidden letters from Maya destabilize both Ravi's authority and Anika's self-punishing narrative.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika reads Maya's plea to choose a life bigger than winning; she starts testing boundaries.

### Chapter 12 - Terms of Return
- **Texture/mechanism:** preparation chapter with restrained dialogue.
- **Objective:** Set conditions for returning to play.
- **Conflict:** Ravi agrees verbally to limits but pushes for old volume in practice details.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika states non-negotiables (rest day, school commitments, pain disclosure), creating a fragile contract.

### Chapter 13 - The Missed Rest Day
- **Texture/mechanism:** domestic rupture chapter.
- **Objective:** Hold her first boundary by taking the agreed rest day.
- **Conflict:** Ravi schedules a secret sparring session and weaponizes Maya's memory.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika refuses and leaves to stay with Priya, making separation visible and costly.

### Chapter 14 - Mediation Table
- **Texture/mechanism:** contained confrontation; leverage shifts.
- **Objective:** Decide whether Anika returns home and to training.
- **Conflict:** Priya forces explicit accounting of grief, debt, and control.
- **Consequence/state change:** A written training agreement is signed (hours cap, third-party oversight, therapy required), partially redistributing power.

### Chapter 15 - Regional Quarterfinal
- **Texture/mechanism:** tactical match + interior counterpoint.
- **Objective:** Compete under the new agreement.
- **Conflict:** Rival Cora exploits Anika's lower volume training; Ravi cannot intervene from the stands.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika loses narrowly but stays physically intact; ambition survives, illusion of invulnerability does not.

### Chapter 16 - Saturday Without a Plan
- **Texture/mechanism:** genuine quiet chapter.
- **Objective:** Get through an ordinary day at home.
- **Conflict:** Without crisis structure, father and daughter struggle to speak honestly.
- **Consequence/state change:** Shared cooking and a messy conversation reopen intimacy; Ravi admits squash became his way to avoid collapsing.

### Chapter 17 - Invitational Semifinal
- **Texture/mechanism:** high-stakes performance + ethical choice.
- **Objective:** Reach the final and secure a sponsor-backed elite slot.
- **Conflict:** Old shin pain returns mid-match; money pressure pushes toward self-harm through play.
- **Consequence/state change:** Anika withdraws on medical advice; sponsor offer is pulled, but father publicly backs her decision.

### Chapter 18 - New Terms
- **Texture/mechanism:** forward-looking aftermath.
- **Objective:** Build a sustainable life after the sponsor loss.
- **Conflict:** Debt remains, grief remains, and Anika misses the narcotic certainty of overtraining.
- **Consequence/state change:** She begins assisting junior clinics while training at humane intensity; grief is integrated as practice, not punishment.
