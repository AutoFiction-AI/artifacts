## Selected Premise
Across two decades of border privatization, a woman who disinfects the toilets and trauma rooms on transcontinental deportation trains works her way up to route supervisor, learning every coupling, restraint protocol, and sewage system on the line. When her estranged son is loaded aboard under a different country's paperwork, she has one season to sabotage the institution she helped keep running.

## Selection Rationale
`candidate_08` won because it has the strongest combination of durability, scene engine, character richness, and consequence range in the field. The premise starts in bodily labor rather than abstraction, builds a long history of complicity, and then detonates that history with a family trigger that is simple, brutal, and dramatically clarifying. It can generate scenes in toilets, trauma rooms, yards, route offices, inspection regimes, detention logistics, and sabotage planning while staying anchored to one compromised protagonist whose decisions matter at personal, institutional, and cross-border scale.

`candidate_03` came closest on raw originality and pressure, but its plant-to-plant quest is more exposed to repetition and thinner secondary bonds. `candidate_06` is genuine risk-taking rather than empty sensationalism because eros, migration, ecology, and firmware all belong to one labor system, but it still feels slightly more vulnerable to episodic drift. `candidate_10` is refreshingly outside the prestige-safe lane and not empty grotesquerie, since the bog-return engine is inseparable from trade, kinship, and grief, yet it depends more on tonal voice than on widening consequence. `candidate_12` is genuinely strong rather than merely literary-looking caution, but it sits closer to the safest historical-literary basin and relies more heavily on execution texture than on the brute, built-in propulsion of its core engine. `candidate_02` deserved protection because its lower-intensity comic-metaphysical mode is real novel material, not a lightweight novelty, but its stakes are more contained than the winner's.
